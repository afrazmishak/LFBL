Become a volunteer

Welcome to our "Become a Volunteer" page! Join us in making a tangible difference in the fight against hunger in our community. As a volunteer at LFBL, you'll play a crucial role in our mission to provide nourishment and hope to those in need. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or want to get involved on a regular basis, there are countless ways to contribute your time and skills.

Why Volunteer?


Make a Direct Impact: Your volunteer efforts directly impact the lives of individuals and families facing food insecurity in our community. Every action you take helps provide essential meals to those in need.


Build Community: Volunteering with us is a great way to connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for helping others. You'll be part of a supportive community dedicated to making a positive difference.


Develop Skills: Volunteering offers valuable opportunities to develop new skills and enhance existing ones. Whether you're interested in logistics, customer service, or event planning, there's a role for you at our food bank.


Flexible Opportunities: We offer a variety of volunteer opportunities to suit your schedule and interests. From sorting and packing food donations to assisting with distribution events, there's a role that's perfect for you.


Feel Good Factor: There's nothing quite like the satisfaction of knowing you've made a meaningful impact on someone's life. Volunteering with us is a rewarding experience that will leave you feeling fulfilled and inspired.


How to become a Volunteer


Sign Up Online: Visit our volunteer portal to browse available opportunities and sign up for shifts that fit your schedule. Click here to register as a Volunteer..


Group Volunteering: Gather your friends, family, or coworkers for a group volunteering experience. It's a fun and impactful way to give back together.


Special Events: Keep an eye out for special events and campaigns where we need extra volunteer support. Whether it's a food drive, fundraiser, or community outreach event, your help is invaluable.


Spread the Word: Help us recruit more volunteers by spreading the word to your network. Share your volunteer experience on social media and encourage others to get involved.

Stay informed and connected! Submit your email address to receive our latest newsfeeds and updates directly to your inbox. Be the first to know about upcoming events, important announcements, and community initiatives. Join our mailing list today and stay engaged with all that's happening.
