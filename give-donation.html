Give Donation

Welcome to our donation page, where every contribution makes a difference in the lives of those facing food insecurity in our community. Your support fuels our mission to provide nourishment and hope to individuals and families in need. With your generosity, we can continue to fight hunger and build a stronger, more resilient community together.

Why Donate?


Make an Impact: Your donation directly supports our efforts to distribute nutritious meals to those who are struggling to put food on the table.


Support Local Families: By donating to our food bank, you're helping your neighbors in need, ensuring they have access to essential food supplies.


Every Dollar Counts: No donation is too small. Even a modest contribution can provide meals for someone in need.


Community Collaboration: When you donate to our food bank, you join a community of caring individuals and organizations committed to ending hunger.


Tax Deductible: Your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.


Ways to Donate


Online Donation: Use our secure online donation form to make a one-time or recurring contribution using your credit card. Click here to make an online donation.


Mail a Check: If you prefer to donate by check, please make it payable to LFBL and mail it to 341 Grange Road, London, SE52 0IZ.


In-Kind Donations We also accept non-perishable food items and other essential supplies. Visit our donation drop-off locations to contribute.


Corporate Giving: Partner with us through corporate donations, sponsorships, or employee matching programs. Contact us to explore partnership opportunities.

Stay informed and connected! Submit your email address to receive our latest newsfeeds and updates directly to your inbox. Be the first to know about upcoming events, important announcements, and community initiatives. Join our mailing list today and stay engaged with all that's happening.
